Welcome to STATMIS flight monitoring app!

STATMIS is an app made for monitoring flight disturbancies and abnormalities. It can be used to check flight frequencies and statictis, as wel.

You can use this app for various needs and hopefully it will fulfill your expectations. Main functionality here is:

  • Basic flight info tracking;
  • Queries to find specific information about known flight data;
  • Some charts and sketches for novice users and those, who don't know what they want;
  • Help (if needed).
