Welcome to flight monitoring app!

This is an app made for monitoring and analyzing flight disturbancies. It can be used to check historical flight data and statictis, as well.

You can use this app for various needs and hopefully it will fulfill your expectations. Main functionality here is:

  • Basic flight info tracking;
  • Statistics about airports operating in Lithuania;
  • Statistics about flights operated by airports of Lithuania;
  • Help and explanations on how to read and interpret provided data
